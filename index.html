The problem

Your insurance company won't call you. We will.

Key insight
After a Santa Ana wind event, heavy rain, or hail — most homeowners assume their roof is fine because they can't see damage from the ground. But hidden flashing and shingle damage qualifies for a full insurance claim. And claim windows close fast.
Your roof is 10+ years old? You may already have qualifying storm or wind damage — even if you haven't had a recent event.
You don't have to use your insurer's contractor. You have the legal right to choose who replaces your roof. We work with your adjuster — not against you.
Claim windows are limited. Most policies require you to file within 12 months of a covered event. Waiting too long means losing coverage — permanently.
A free inspection costs you nothing. Our inspectors identify damage invisible from the ground. No findings means no obligation — and no cost to you.
